01  —  Philosophy

At STRP LAB, we believe a watch is never truly finished.
It evolves with its owner.

Every strap is an extension of personality, taste, and identity.

Crafted from premium materials and engineered for exceptional fit, our products transform iconic timepieces into something uniquely yours. Each piece begins as an idea — then becomes an obsession.

We source only materials that age with dignity. Vulcanised rubber, full-grain leather, and brushed titanium hardware. Built to outlast trends, built for the collector who understands that details are everything.

    Material Selection

    We source only the finest vulcanised rubber, full-grain leather, and aerospace-grade titanium. Every material is tested for longevity and feel before it reaches your wrist.
